Welcome to Our Virtual Classroom

Outlined below are our classroom expectations and attendance policy for all students. Parents and students alike should review this document before beginning tutoring.

Classroom Expectations

Preparedness

Students should come to class ready to learn with all necessary materials. Class assignments should be submitted to the Dropbox at least 24 hours in advance of class. This gives the tutor time to review the assignment and better prepare for class. Materials sent on short notice (<24 hours) may go unseen.

Attendance Policy

To ensure students stay on track, it is important that they show up to tutoring. Those who do not risk falling behind. If your student plans to miss tutoring, please cancel in advance.

Cancel or Reschedule

To cancel or reschedule an appointment, open the invitation email you received for the appointment you booked. Scroll to the bottom of the email, and select “cancel” or “reschedule”.

Notice of Cancellation

Students must give a minimum 6-hour notice for cancellation. Cancellations can be made through the invitation email. Cancellations made <6 hours before class starts will be assessed a $15.00 late cancellation fee.

Tardiness/Absence Policy

Students should come to class on time. Students who come late will not have class extended to accommodate for their tardiness.

If a student does not show within 15 minutes of the start time of the session, the session will be canceled and a $15.00 late attendance fee will be assessed on top of the student’s hourly rate.

Students that regularly reschedule appointments may be declined future tutoring. Please plan to make the session at the time and date that you schedule.

**Exceptions for emergencies will be made on a case-by-case basis.
