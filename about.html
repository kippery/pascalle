Our Origin

Founded at the peak of the pandemic in 2020, Pascalle was conceptualized as a response to the sudden shift in educational landscapes. Recognizing the pressing need for a reliable means of virtual tutoring, Pascalle swiftly rose to the occasion, becoming a premiere educational resource for students across America.

Our Tutors

At Pascalle, we understand that the foundation of exceptional virtual tutoring lies in the expertise of our tutors. Every tutor within our network is meticulously vetted for their mathematical prowess, ensuring that students receive guidance from industry-qualified professionals committed to their success.

Our Areas of Expertise

Today, Pascalle stands proudly as a beacon of mathematical excellence, providing students with a comprehensive suite of virtual tutoring services. We specialize in a range of subjects, including:

Grades 9-12

  • Algebra I
  • Algebra II
  • Geometry
  • Probability
  • Statistics
  • Precalculus / Trigonometry

College Bound

  • SAT Math
  • AP Calculus AB

Join Pascalle on the journey to academic excellence, where virtual tutoring transcends boundaries, subjects come to life, and success becomes a virtual reality.
